How to read this report

ENCAP does not make final determinations about truthfulness. Instead, it highlights signals that may correlate with misinformation. Combine these indicators with your own domain knowledge, additional sources, and editorial judgment.

  • Use the score as a prioritization signal, not a verdict.
  • Review each module section to understand which factors drove the score.
  • Document decisions for accountability and future audits.
