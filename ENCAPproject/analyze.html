What ENCAP evaluates

The analysis pipeline is built around multiple, interpretable modules. Each module contributes its own perspective to the final credibility score and report.

  • Source credibility – Checks whether the domain appears in trusted or repeatedly problematic outlet lists.
  • Sensational language – Detects emotional, exaggerated, or manipulative phrasing.
  • Headline structure – Flags clickbait or overly vague headline constructions.
  • Claim repetition – Compares extracted claims to a history of previously flagged narratives.
